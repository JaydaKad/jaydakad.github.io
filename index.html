About Me

I am a scientist, a keen traveller and an excellent baker! I love to immerse myself in different cultures and languages when travelling. I’ve been lucky enough to be able to combine my love of travelling and learning in the past, having both taught and attended language courses abroad. I speak Spanish and Turkish at an intermediate level and, with the use of technology, continue to practice.
